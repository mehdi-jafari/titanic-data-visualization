Selected Variables Distribution

In this project, I am analyzing and visualizing the demographics and passenger information from 891 of the 2224 passengers and crew on board the Titanic. I will try to answer the following questions:

  • Did people's socioeconomic status help them to get into a lifeboat?
  • How did being with family affect their chance for survival? What about gender and age?

    • The above depicted charts are the distribution of the following variables in the dataset: Gender, Socio-economic, Age, Traveling Alone.

    Selected Variables Distribution Grouped by Survival Status


    The charts represent the distribution of perished and survived passengers for each selected variable

    Selected Variables Distribution Grouped by Survival Status In Percentage

    The charts represent the same distribution as the previous step except that it is in percentage.
